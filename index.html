About

I'm an adult psychiatrist and psychotherapist licensed to see patients in New York State. Because I'm trained in both psychotherapy and medication management, I can provide integrated care, making your treatment more cohesive and effective.

Often high achieving people find that on the road to success, they have lost touch with important parts of their core self. I enjoy helping people explore and nurture these neglected parts and find deeper connection to their own creativity, their loved ones, and their community.

I'd be happy to speak with you more to see if we are a good fit. You can sign up for a free phone consultation here. I am currently seeing patients virtually.

Services

Psychiatric Evaluation
In our first session, you can expect thoughtful, attentive listening as you share what brings you in. Too often in the medical system, we rush to treat without a thorough understanding of the problem at hand. My goal is for you to feel heard, respected, and understood. Depending on your needs and goals, I may recommend therapy with medication, therapy only, or medication only.

Medication Management
I take a conservative and thoughtful approach to prescribing. I also have experience in safe deprescribing and simplifying complex medication regimens.

Psychodynamic Psychotherapy
Psychodynamic psychotherapy examines how past experiences shape our current conflicts. Together, we can foster an open, curious space where we explore your inner world and get to the root of your struggles.

Exposure and Response Prevention Therapy
EXRP is the gold standard treatment for OCD. It can be particularly effective in combination with medication. I am also interested in working with patients who would like to transition from exposure-based work to more exploratory work.
